How old are you on other planets?

Did you know that if you were born and lived on a different planet you'd have a different age? Yeah, yeah I know you can't actually be born and live on different planets but stick with me here. A year is defined as the orbital period of a planetary body. Basically, how long it takes a planet to make one complete orbit around the Sun.

The planets have the following year lengths in comparison to the Earth:

  • Mercury: 0.24 Earth years
  • Venus: 0.62 Earth years
  • Mars: 1.88 Earth years
  • Jupiter: 11.86 Earth years
  • Saturn: 29.46 Earth years
  • Uranus: 84.02 Earth years
  • Neptune: 164.8 Earth years
